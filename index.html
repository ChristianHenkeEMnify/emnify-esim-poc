EMnify eSIM Setup

Follow the two steps below to activate your SIM

1
Install APN Settings

This installs the network configuration so your device connects to the EMnify network correctly.

Install APN Settings

After tapping, iOS will show a "Profile Downloaded" notification. Go to Settings → General → VPN & Device Management and tap Install. Then return here for Step 2.

2
Install eSIM

Open your Camera app and point it at the QR code below to install your eSIM profile.
